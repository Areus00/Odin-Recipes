Basic Beef Burger

Need a basic beef burger whipped up in no time? We have the perfect one

ingredients:

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Directions:

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly pil grate
  2. Whisk together egg, salt, and pepper in an medium bowl
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix with your hands and fork untill well blended
  4. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties
  5. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
