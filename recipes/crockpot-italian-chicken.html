Crockpot Italian Chicken

Simple dish requiring few ingredients, 5min prep time and a 8hr cooking period.It is an adequete serving for 4 people

ingredients:

  • 1 bottle italian dressing
  • 1/4 cup of grated paramese cheese
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions:

  1. Stir italian dressing and paramese cheese in a bowl
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker, and pour dressing mixture over chicken. Cover and cook on low untill chicken is tender, no longer pink in the centre, and juice runs clear, about 8 hours.
